Privacy Policy

Summary: Beauty Board respects your privacy. We only collect data necessary for the app to function and never share it with third parties for commercial purposes.

1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Beauty Board ("we", "our", "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile management application for beauty salons.

2. Data Collected

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Salon information (name, address, phone, business ID)

2.2 Business Data

  • Client list and contact information
  • Appointments and history
  • Services and pricing
  • Payments and receipts

2.3 Google Calendar Data

If you enable Google Calendar synchronization, we access:

  • Your calendar events (read and write)
  • To display your availability and sync appointments
Important: Google Calendar access is optional. You can use Beauty Board without this feature. We only access data necessary for appointment synchronization.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Manage your appointments and clients
  • Generate receipts and statistics
  • Sync with Google Calendar (if enabled)
  • Securely backup your data
  • Send reminder notifications (if enabled)

4. Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform)
  • Encrypted transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Authentication
  • Sensitive data stored in iOS Keychain
  • Access restricted by Firestore security rules

5. Data Sharing

We never sell your data.

Your data may only be shared with:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: hosting and storage
  • Google Calendar: synchronization (if enabled by you)

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate information
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

7. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • After account deletion, data is erased within 30 days
  • Receipts may be retained per legal requirements (up to 10 years)

8. Cookies and Trackers

The Beauty Board app does not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers. Only technical identifiers necessary for operation are used.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated. In case of significant changes, you will be notified through the app.

10. Google API Services User Data Policy

Beauty Board's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
